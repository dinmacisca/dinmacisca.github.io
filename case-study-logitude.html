Project Timeline
How the Operations System Was Built
APR 2022
Baseline Assessment
Audited existing project reporting across all active sites including Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Awka. Found data was tracked in 6 separate unlinked spreadsheets with no standard format across departments.
JUN 2022
Centralised Dashboard Built
Developed a master Excel tracker with automated budget variance formulas and RAG (Red/Amber/Green) conditional formatting. Integrated into Power BI for executive-level reporting across all sites.
SEP 2022
First Major Inconsistency Flagged
Identified a ₦4.2M procurement discrepancy at Lekki Phase 1 site through variance analysis — 6 weeks before the quarterly management review. Escalated and resolved within 3 weeks.
MAR 2023
Cross-Department Protocol Introduced
Rolled out weekly data check-ins between construction, procurement, and finance teams across all site locations including Port Harcourt. Reduced reconciliation delays from 3 weeks to 4 days.
DEC 2023
Annual Operations Report Delivered
Final report presented to management showing 18% reduction in budget variance and 31% improvement in on-time project milestone delivery since dashboard implementation.
Key Findings
01
Lekki Phase 1 had an undetected ₦4.2M procurement discrepancy
Caught through budget variance tracking 6 weeks before the quarterly review. Escalated and resolved before it impacted project delivery.
02
Duplicate supplier invoicing was the most common issue (9 cases)
Identified by cross-referencing procurement logs against payment records. Finance audit protocol implemented as a preventive measure.
03
Port Harcourt site had the best budget adherence of all locations
Attributed to focused site scope and strong local supplier relationships. Recommended as a model for other site operations.
04
QuickPools projects had the fastest delivery cycle of all project types
Including the JK Randle complex — swimming pool projects consistently finished within or under timeline. Recommended as priority category for new client acquisition pitches.
05
Suspended pool projects required specialist timeline management
Second-floor and elevated pool installations required longer structural preparation phases. Developed a separate timeline template for these project types to improve scheduling accuracy.
